Frequently Asked Questions

What's your return policy?

You can return unworn items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Simply contact us to initiate the process.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within Europe takes 3–5 business days. Express options are available at checkout.

How do I find my size?

Our shoes run true to European sizing. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for hiking and trail styles.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 47 countries. International shipping times and costs vary — see checkout for details.
