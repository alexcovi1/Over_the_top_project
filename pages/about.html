ABOUT OVER THE TOP

Crafted for adventurers, built for excellence

Our Story

Over The Top was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves shoes that match their passion for adventure. Whether you're running through city streets, hiking mountain trails, or conquering extreme terrain, we create footwear that empowers you to push beyond limits.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted brand for outdoor enthusiasts worldwide, we've remained committed to quality, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To design and manufacture premium footwear that enables athletes and explorers to achieve their goals on any terrain while maintaining the highest standards of comfort and durability.

Our Vision

To become the world's most respected footwear brand for outdoor enthusiasts, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and our unwavering commitment to customer excellence.

Our Values

Quality, innovation, sustainability, and integrity guide everything we do. We believe in pushing boundaries while respecting nature and communities around us.
